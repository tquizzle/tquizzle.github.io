There have been a lack of updates here on my website lately due to a problem I alluded to a couple of months ago. It&#8217;s been slightly debilitating, immensely aggravating, and an all-around crappy six months.

A couple of weeks ago I finally got to have carpal tunnel release surgery on my left hand. I hope this proves hugely successful. If it does, the right hand is next as I have been diagnosed with carpal tunnel syndrome in both hands. So for the time being, I am Mr. Righty.

I never would have thought it would be so difficult to live life with numb hands, but now living that life is so surreal.

Things will continue to be quite light around here until I get both hands up to snuff, and that may take a while&#8230;its kind of hard to code one handed.
